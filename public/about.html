About Me

Hello! I’m a passionate data enthusiast with a strong academic foundation in computer applications and science. I completed my Master of Computer Applications (MCA) from NCRD's Sterling Institute of Management Studies, Mumbai, and hold a Bachelor of Science (BSc) from a college affiliated with Shivaji University, Kolhapur.

During my academic journey, I discovered my knack for data analytics and problem-solving. One of my key projects, "Bank Analytics," involved leveraging tools like Power BI and Tableau to analyze loan data and customer trends, providing actionable insights to optimize business decisions. I thrive on turning raw data into meaningful stories that drive growth and innovation.

When I’m not exploring data, I enjoy embracing new challenges, learning emerging technologies, and contributing to projects that make an impact. With a mix of technical expertise and a results-oriented mindset, I’m always looking to collaborate on ideas that create value.
